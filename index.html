Privacy Policy for Masareef

Last updated: 5 July 2026

Masareef ("the app") is a personal expense tracker for Android. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and why. In plain terms: Masareef is offline-first and built to keep your financial data on your device.

Our core principle

Your transactions, wallets, budgets, and categories are stored locally on your device. The app works fully without an account and without internet. We never sell your data, there are no ads, and amounts, merchant names, and balances are never sent to analytics. Drafts that Auto-track reads from your bank notifications stay in a local inbox on your device and are never uploaded — even when you sync.

Notification access (optional, off by default)

If you enable Auto-track, Masareef reads incoming payment and transaction notifications from banks and payment apps to create expense records automatically. This reading happens entirely on your device. The content of your notifications is never transmitted to us or any third party. The drafts this creates ("captures") are kept in a local review inbox on your device only — they are never uploaded or synced, even if you sign in to sync. You can disable Auto-track at any time in Settings, which stops all notification access.

Location (optional, only for Nearby ATMs)

When you open the Nearby ATMs screen, the app requests your device location once to find ATMs near you. Your coordinates are sent to the OpenStreetMap Overpass API (overpass-api.de), a public map-data service, solely to retrieve nearby ATM locations for that request. Your location is not stored by the app and is not used for any other purpose. Location is only accessed while you are using this feature; the app never tracks your location in the background.

Optional account & sync

If you choose to create an account and sign in, your app data (such as transactions, wallets, budgets, and categories) is synchronised to our backend so you can access it across devices. This is entirely optional — if you never sign in, no financial data leaves your device. Account data is protected by your login and transmitted over encrypted connections. You can request deletion of your account and its synced data by contacting us at the email below.

Usage analytics

To understand how Masareef is used and to improve it, the app collects non-financial, aggregate usage analytics — for example, which screen you opened or that a transaction was added. We do not send transaction amounts, merchant names, balances, currencies, account numbers, notification contents, or your location to analytics. Analytics are processed on our behalf by PostHog, on servers located in the European Union.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers.
  • We do not access your SMS or call logs in the Google Play version of the app.
  • We do not track your location in the background.

Children

Masareef is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Your rights

You can delete all local data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling it. If you use account sync, contact us to request deletion of your synced data.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
