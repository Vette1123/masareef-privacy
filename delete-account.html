Delete Your Account & Data

Masareef — developed by Mohamed Gado

This page explains how to request deletion of your Masareef account and the data associated with it. Masareef is a personal spending tracker. Most of your data (transactions, wallets, budgets and categories) is stored only on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

How to request account deletion

  1. Email boogado@yahoo.com from the email address you used to sign in to Masareef.
  2. Use the subject line Delete my Masareef account.
  3. We verify the request against your account email and delete your account within 30 days. We reply to confirm once it is done.

Remove data stored on your device

Because your financial records live locally, you can delete them yourself at any time — no request needed:

  • Uninstall the app, or
  • Go to Android Settings → Apps → Masareef → Storage → Clear storage.

This permanently removes all local transactions, wallets, budgets and categories from your device.

What is kept

We do not retain any personal data after your account is deleted, except where a limited retention period is required by law (for example, records tied to fraud prevention or legal obligation). No transaction amounts, merchant names, balances or account numbers are ever stored on our servers.
