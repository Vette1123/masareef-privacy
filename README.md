# Masareef — Privacy Policy

Privacy policy for **Masareef**, a private, offline-first expense tracker for Android.

📄 **Live policy:** https://vette1123.github.io/masareef-privacy/

## About Masareef

Masareef tracks your spending automatically by reading your bank's payment
notifications **on your device** — no daily data entry. It's offline-first,
has no ads, works in any country and currency, and keeps your financial data
on your phone.

- Automatic spending capture from bank notifications (opt-in, on-device)
- Categories, budgets, multiple wallets, recurring transactions
- Monthly & all-time insights
- Nearby ATMs (foreground location, only when you open that screen)
- Optional account sync — the app is fully functional without it

## Privacy in one line

Your money data lives on your device. No ads. Amounts, merchants, and balances
are never sent to analytics.

The full policy is in [`index.html`](index.html) and published via GitHub Pages
at the link above.
